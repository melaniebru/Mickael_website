What's new?
We finished our paper with David Dereudre, Adrien Hardy and Mylène Maïda, proving DLR equations for the Sine-\(\beta\) process, and its rigidity. Building on this "physical" characterization, I prove a central limit theorem for the fluctuations of linear statistics in Sine-\(\beta\).
