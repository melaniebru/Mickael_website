ODE - References.

  • A good set of lecture notes. 1,2,3 are relevant.

  • An elementary, complete proof of Hartman-Grobman.
  • A book on numerical methods for ODE's. We have covered a subset of 1,2,3,4,5,6 but this has a lot more details.
  • Great lecture notes on numerical analysis.
  • Huge list of interesting exercises (Qual exams from U. Maryland). Not all of them are on topics we have covered, and many of them are non trivial!
